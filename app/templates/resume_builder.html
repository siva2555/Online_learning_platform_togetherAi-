How to Build a Strong Resume

Follow these steps to create a compelling resume:

Step 1: Contact Information

Include your name, phone number, email, and LinkedIn profile.

Step 2: Write a Strong Summary

Summarize your skills, experience, and career goals in 2-3 sentences.

Step 3: Work Experience

List your previous jobs, including company name, job title, and responsibilities.

Step 4: Education

Include your degrees, certifications, and relevant coursework.

Step 5: Skills

Mention both technical and soft skills that are relevant to your job.

Step 6: Projects

Add details about projects you've worked on, including achievements.
